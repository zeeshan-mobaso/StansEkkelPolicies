Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How do I play?
A: Tap on screen to shoot — your goal is to kill the target enemies.

Q: Does the game require an internet connection?
A: No. The entire game works offline.

Q: Can I reset or start a new puzzle?
A: Yes! Tap the “Pause” buttons anytime to begin again from panel.
